Definitions

  1. "Practically free" or "practically eliminate college textbook costs" refers to the possibility that your textbooks could be free. This depends on a number of circumstances based on your individual effort, participation, availability, and ability to comprehend, follow, and apply the system.
  2. "The system" is the way the web site is set up to enable efficient transfer of assets (textbooks), which leads to textbooks having the potential of being "practically free."
