Getting Started

  1. Educate yourself by learning more.
  2. Watch an online tutorial which demonstrates the search, barter trade, and contact a seller feautures of CollegeBookEvolution.com.
  3. Join the Evolution, become a Member!
  4. List your books
    • When you login, navigate to the "Books" tab, to add a book, find the class your book was for, and select your book. Then proceed by selecting the condition and ask price. Be sure to be honest about your books condition, you will be meeting on campus with the buyer to make the exchange you and the buyer agree upon. In order to list a textbook you will need to have purchased textbook credits. If the book or class is not listed, please suggest it by providing us with its information.
  5. Add your classes
    • When you login, navigate to the "Classes" tab, to add a class, simply select the appropriate fields and select "Add Class." If the class or book is not listed, please suggest it by providing us with its information.
