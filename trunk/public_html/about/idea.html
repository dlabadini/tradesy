The Big Idea

A current West Virginia University student who was struggling financially had become overwhelmingly frustrated with the textbook situation on campus. After buying a book from a friend to save money, he instantly realized he was on to something.

Immediately he evolved his idea by building a web site that empowers the students. CollegeBookEvolution.com is designed to facilitate, match, and connect college students with each other. The website features an optimal pricing system which is foundation for enabling your college books to be practically free.
