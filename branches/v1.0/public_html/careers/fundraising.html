    Marketing Team ("Agent")
Our passion for empowering college students goes beyond just saving them a ridiculous amount of money on textbooks; we believe in giving back. If you are a part of a large student organization (Fraternity, Sorority, Club) on your campus it may qualify to become a Marketing Team for CollegeBookEvolution, LLC. Marketing Teams help advertise and manage your Universities CollegeBookEvolution.com webpage in various ways; in return the Marketing Team will receive a portion of the subscription fee for each student who signs up with their Marketing Team's coupon code.

If your organization is accepted as a Marketing Team, the organization will have to go through a minimum one week "CollegeBookEvolution.com Marketing Team Coaching Program."



CollegeBookEvolution.com Marketing Team Coaching Program focuses on:
  • Foundational skills and knowledge
  • Becoming a team
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Organizing tasks
  • Web site training
  • Activities and expectations
  • Final assessments
