    Campus Director ("Agent")
Only highly motivated individuals who have incredible people and time management skills are considered for this position. Campus Director must have extensive knowledge of the business and college campus. The main responsibility of the Campus Director is to train, lead, and supervise the Marketing Team. Additionally, the Campus Director is challenged with the task of finding advocates and gaining support of school officials and organizations (SGA, Student Affairs, etc). Furthermore, the campus director acts as a secretary for CollegeBookEvolution.com, LLC for their campus.

If you are accepted as a Campus Director, you will have to go through a minimum three week "CollegeBookEvolution.com Campus Director Coaching Program." You will shadow a current Marketing Director, and during that time adjust a suggested Marketing Campaign to your specific college campus. Marketing Directors receive a portion of the subscription fee for each student who signs up under their Marketing Team's "coupon code."



CollegeBookEvolution.com Campus Director Coaching Program focuses on:
  • Foundational skills and knowledge
  • Becoming a leader and motivator
  • Team choice, management, and development
  • Resourcing the campus, organizations, and faculty
  • Learning ways to assist the Marketing Team
  • Implementing new ideas and approaches
  • Marketing development and strategies
  • Extensive web site training
  • Activities, expectations, procedures
  • Final assessments
